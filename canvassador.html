What is the Empower Canvassador program?

Thanks for your interest in our exciting new Empower Canvassador program!

The program's primary mission is to empower content creators who consistently create engaging content and tutorials around Canva's products and features.

Empower Canvassadors excel in simplifying complex tasks on Canva, creating a variety of tutorials around our products & features that make it easy for others to understand and upskill effortlessly. Program members gain access to valuable resources that will support and enhance their content creation efforts.

Together, we will work towards empowering the world to unleash their creativity through design!

Who makes a great Empower Canvassador?

We're looking for community members who consistently create engaging social media content and tutorials around Canva's products and features.

  • Live the Canva Values
  • Be an independent creator (not a brand or agency)
  • Have an active and engaged social media audience
  • Create and share at least one Canva-related tutorial or feature each month
  • Agree to abide by Canva's branding and NDA guidelines

Which platforms are we currently working with?

We are currently collaborating with community members who create content on the following social platforms:

  • Instagram
  • Twitter
  • TikTok
  • YouTube
  • Facebook
  • LinkedIn

We'll begin to add more channels as the program evolves!

What are some of the perks of joining?

These are just some of the things our Empower Canvassadors get to enjoy while being a part of the program:

  • Special Canvassador badge
  • Opportunities to participate in beta tests
  • Educational workshops and webinars
  • Highlights featuring recent releases and updates
  • Affiliate perks (earn commissions on Canva Pro conversions)

Additional benefits will be added along the way!
